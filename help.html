How to Play Battleships

This version Battleships is a mix between the classic Battleships game and Sudoku.
The purpose of the game is to fill up the board with water and ships so that the location of every ship is known.

  

Initial Problem           Completed Board

There a 4 different types of ships:

 One Battleship

 Two Cruisers

 Three Destroyers

 Four Submarines

The numbers on the side of the rows and columns indicate the number of pieces of a ship in that specific row or column.

One last rule is that no part of a ship may touch adjacently nor diagonally with another ship.
